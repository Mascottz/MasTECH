// Services

What I can handle for your business.

I combine web design and practical IT support so you don’t have to manage multiple people for simple tech needs. Whether you’re a small business, freelancer or NGO in Abuja, I help you stay online, reachable and running smoothly.

Business & Landing Page Websites

Clean, responsive websites that explain who you are, what you offer and how customers can contact you. Ideal for real estate agents, restaurants, salons, coaches and other service‑based businesses.

  • – Single‑page or multi‑page business sites
  • – Mobile‑friendly layouts that load fast
  • – WhatsApp and “call now” call‑to‑action buttons
  • – WordPress setup and simple e‑commerce if needed

Simple Web Apps & PHP/MySQL

Small custom systems like job boards, dashboards or internal tools built with PHP and MySQL, tailored to how you already work instead of forcing you into a one‑size‑fits‑all template.

  • – User logins and roles (admin, staff, public)
  • – Data entry forms and simple reporting
  • – Lightweight admin panels for managing content
  • – Local development with XAMPP, deploy‑ready structure

Laptop Repair & IT Support

Practical support for your everyday tech: from slow or faulty laptops to basic office setups and internet configuration. I focus on simple explanations and quick, realistic solutions.

  • – Laptop & desktop diagnostics and repair
  • – RAM, SSD and battery upgrades
  • – OS installation, cleanup and virus removal
  • – Basic networking and small office / home setups in Abuja
