About Me

I’m a Computer Hardware Engineer and Web Developer based in Abuja FCT. I help individuals and small businesses fix their tech and build a professional online presence that actually supports their goals.

On the hardware side, I diagnose and repair laptop and desktop issues, handle upgrades (RAM, SSD, battery, keyboard), remove viruses and set up proper backups so your important files stay safe. I focus on honest feedback, realistic pricing and clear communication.

On the web side, I design and build clean, responsive websites using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, WordPress and simple backend tools. My focus is on small businesses that need a site that looks modern, loads fast and makes it easy for customers to contact them via WhatsApp, phone or email.

Right now I’m rebuilding my portfolio with fresh demo projects, including a real estate layout and a restaurant layout, to show different styles I can deliver for clients in Abuja and beyond.
