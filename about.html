// About MasTECH Innovations

Web design and tech support for everyday businesses in Abuja.

MasTECH Innovations is my small web and IT studio based in Abuja, Nigeria. I help business owners who don’t have an in‑house “IT guy” get reliable support for both their computers and their online presence.

I started by repairing laptops and desktops for friends and local businesses. Over time, many of those same clients needed simple, clean websites to look more professional and attract customers. Today, I combine both skills: I build responsive sites and keep the tech behind them running smoothly.

On the hardware side, I handle diagnostics, repairs and upgrades like RAM, SSDs and batteries, plus virus removal and fresh OS installs. On the web side, I design and build modern websites and small PHP/MySQL applications tailored to how your business actually works.

If you’re in Abuja and you want someone you can reach easily on WhatsApp, who explains things in simple language and focuses on practical results, MasTECH was created for you.
