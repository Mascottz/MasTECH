// About MasTECH Innovations

Web design and tech support for everyday businesses in Abuja.

MasTECH Innovations is my small web and IT studio based in Abuja, Nigeria. I help business owners who don’t have an in‑house “IT person” get reliable support for both their computers and their online presence.

I started by repairing laptops and desktops for friends, neighbours and local businesses. Over time, those same people began asking for simple, clean websites – a place online where customers could see what they do, find their contact details and trust that they are serious. That’s how the web design side of MasTECH began.

Today, I combine both worlds: I still handle diagnostics, repairs and upgrades like RAM, SSDs and batteries, plus virus removal and fresh OS installs. At the same time, I design and build modern websites, small PHP/MySQL applications and even little tools like habit trackers and interactive simulators to solve specific problems.

My focus is on clear communication and practical results. I prefer simple, fast sites over complicated dashboards that nobody uses. Most of my work is for small businesses and individuals around Abuja who want someone they can message easily on WhatsApp, who explains things in plain English and doesn’t disappear after the first payment.

If that sounds like the kind of person you want to handle your website or your everyday tech, you can explore my portfolio or get in touch here.
